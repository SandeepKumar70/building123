Smart Design Conclave & Awards

Organized By Building Material Reporter

Smart Design Conclave & Awards (SDCA) is a dynamic half-day event dedicated to empowering local talent and driving excellence in design and architecture, with a special focus on Tier-2 cities. It serves as a platform to showcase cutting-edge building materials and technological advancements, fostering innovation in the industry. By bringing together professionals, thought leaders, and visionaries, SDCA contributes to India's national progress, shaping a more aesthetically enriched and future-ready built environment.
MANTHAN: A Discussion on Cultural Preservation and Redevelopment The panel discussion, Manthan, revolved around the theme of "Unity through Design: Redevelopment of Awadh While Preserving Cultural Heritage." The conversation underscored the need for a balanced approach to urban development that respects Awadh's rich cultural history while addressing modern infrastructure needs. The panel concluded with a call to action for architects to adopt inclusive design strategies that celebrate heritage while driving urban growth. Panellists agreed that collaboration across disciplines is essential in creating spaces that honour tradition yet foster innovation.
Keynote Addresses Ar. Vivek Singh Rathore, Design Principal of Salient Design Studio and Ar. Shirish Beri of Shirish Beri & Associates were among the highlights. Ar. Rathore's presentation, Shift To Sustain, emphasised the urgent need for sustainability in architecture, advocating for reuse and restoration. Ar. Beri's talk, Materiality and Space, focused on using nature as a material in design, stressing the importance of integrating the local environment without disrupting ecological balance.
Industry Leaders Meet Influencers The event was made possible through the generous support of several industry leaders, whose contributions were vital in creating a platform for knowledge sharing and networking. The sponsors, including Haique, FIMA, KEI, Gujarat Guardian, NewMika, Alstone, VOX, and Vinayak Fenester, showcased their latest products and technologies, offering the attendees a glimpse into the future of architecture and design.
